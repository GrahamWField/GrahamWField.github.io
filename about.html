About Me

Hello! I'm Graham Field, a certified Data Analyst based in Rotterdam, The Netherlands. Beyond my professional life, I am passionate about the great outdoors and watersports, finding joy and exhilaration in activities like fishing, sailing, and hiking. My love for nature is matched by my enthusiasm for gaming and computers, where I indulge my curiosity and continually seek out new challenges and knowledge.

Naturally inquisitive from a young age, I've always been eager to explore and understand the world around me. This curiosity fuels my work in data analytics, where I delve deep into data to uncover insights and solve complex problems. Whether navigating the open waters or decoding a challenging dataset, I approach each task with dedication and a desire to learn and grow.

Background

I was born in the vibrant and lively city of Johannesburg, where I spent my younger years attending Grayston Prep. It was here that my passion for sailing took root, and I dedicated myself to the sport, eventually achieving provincial colours. Growing up in such a dynamic city, I was surrounded by a diverse group of neighborhood friends, each contributing to a rich tapestry of experiences that shaped my formative years.

For high school, I attended the boarding school Saint Andrew's School in Bloemfontein. Steeped in tradition, Saint Andrew's provided a wide range of academic, cultural, and athletic opportunities. During my time there, I played rugby, hockey, cricket, and squash, and also participated in debating and chess, embracing the holistic education and vibrant community the school offered.

I then attended the University of the Free State for two years, pursuing a degree in Business & Marketing Management. However, I was soon drawn into the world of work, where I found my passion for practical application and hands-on experience, leading me to prioritize career development over academic pursuits.

In Johannesburg, I began my professional journey working in the luxury goods sector, specifically with the prestigious brand TAG Heuer at Picot and Moss. Here, I gained valuable experience in retail management, stock control, marketing, and training, which provided a solid foundation for my career.

Subsequently, I transitioned into entrepreneurship, focusing on community development and agriculture. I founded and managed Urban Fresh, a fresh produce business that aimed to empower local communities through sustainable agricultural practices and market access programs. This venture allowed me to expand customer and supplier networks, optimize logistics, and drive community development initiatives, ultimately fostering significant business growth and strengthened relationships within the community.

After several successful years in Johannesburg, I decided to move to Europe, specifically the Netherlands, with the intention of exploring Europe and developing a new chapter in my life. This move was also driven by a desire to be closer to family settled in the Netherlands. The rich cultural heritage and the vibrant opportunities in Europe have provided an ideal environment for both personal and professional growth.

Having thoroughly enjoyed the travel and freedom afforded by my role as a Wind Turbine Technician, I am now excited to settle down in the Netherlands and embark on a new chapter in data analytics.
